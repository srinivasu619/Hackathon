India

India and Indian civilization have played a major part in human development, world history and international relations. With roughly 1.2 billion people, India is the world’s largest democracy and second largest country by population.


  • Population: 1.2 billion people (2010)
  • Total area: 1.269 million sq. miles (3.287 million sq. km)
  • Capital city: New Delhi
  • GDP per capita (International $, PPP): $3,270 (2009)
  • GDP annual growth rate: 6.0% (1980-2009), 8.2% (forecast, 2011-2015)
  • Life expectancy at birth: 63.7 years
